# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Industrial and commercial decision-makers in Indonesia/Southeast Asia — facility managers, building owners, engineering consultants, and procurement teams looking for HVAC, chiller, solar, and green energy solutions. They value technical credibility, reliability, and sustainability credentials.

## Product Purpose

PT Benua Green Energy's website exists to establish the company as the trusted green technology partner for industrial energy needs — converting technical authority into qualified leads. Success means visitors leave with confidence that BGE can deliver their energy infrastructure projects.

## Brand Personality

**Terpercaya, Industrial, Hijau** — trustworthy, technical, and green-tech-forward. Authoritative without being cold. Modern without being trendy. Speaks with the confidence of 23+ years in the industry.

## Anti-references

- WordPress Elementor template sites (overused layouts, generic icons, bloated footers)
- Over-polished SaaS landing pages (B2B industrial needs to feel substantial, not lightweight)
- AI-generated "creative agency" style (gradient text, glassmorphism for no reason, sketchy illustrations)
- Dark navy "corporate" clones (every industrial site uses navy — BGE owns green)

## Design Principles

1. **Green is the identity, not the accent** — the brand is green tech; green carries the primary visual weight, not navy or gray
2. **Technical credibility first** — product imagery, specifications, and real metrics (CO2 avoided, years experience) drive trust
3. **Show the hardware** — chiller plants, solar panels, industrial equipment are hero assets, not abstract icons
4. **One brand, one system** — consistent typography, color, spacing across every page
5. **Motion with purpose** — micro-interactions guide attention and convey precision, not decoration

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (4.5:1 body text contrast)
- Reduced motion respected on all animations
- Focus states visible on all interactive elements
- Language toggle for Indonesian/English
